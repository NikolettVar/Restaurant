Meet the founder

The Blue Stag was created in 2008 by executive chef John Murphy. Born and raised in Dublin, Ireland, John holds more than 10 years of restaurant experience.

Our History

It all started in 2008 in Dublin City, when John's entrepreneurial spirit and desire to create unforgettable food led to the opening of The Blue Stag. Since then, through word of mouth, a lot of hard work and a commitment to offering the finest food in town, The Blue Stag has become one of Dublin's top restaurants.

Our Mission

At The Blue Stag, we are commmited to offer the finest dining experience in Dublin. We believe that using the freshest, locally sourced organic ingredients give the best results.
